Projects

Bibloskart

Bibloskart is an e-commerce app where users can buy or rent old as well as new books. Renting option has been great in the offline market and now we are launching it in online market as well. Find the exciting offer in our app on over thousand of products. Bibloskart is one of India's leading platform of book services. Not only restricted to this services, Bibloskart provides scope to sell old and used books via the app.

WallX

WallX is a wallpaper app with beautiful material design. This app was built with a mindset that the user should engage with the app as soon as he opens it and should be instantly get hooked to it because of the usability and beautiful looking UI. The app currently has around 2200+ downloads. A wallpaper app that will showcase the latest and most magnificent wallpapers and the best part is it's completely free.

Don't Remember

Don't Remember is a note and reminder app which will help you out with mundane tasks that we tend to forget. The app has a unique and attractive design which will make the user fell in love with the app. Also, the best part is everything is synced to the cloud, which will make switching devices easier without losing any of your precious data.

5wasara

5wasara is an educational app which brings question papers to help 5th-grade students achieve betters marks for their scholarship exam in Sri Lanka. Currently, the app consists of 7 original, updated, and unique question papers. Each one of them are of 45 minutes. And the app currently has over 5000+ downloads.

Music Player

Music Player app was built with the intention to offer a simple UI to users who are interested in listening to music and do not want to fiddle around with the app searching for the very basic things. Not having too much stuff on the screen helps users focus on the key things. Hence the app looks simple but is very functional and minimalistic.
